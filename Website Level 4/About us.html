About Us

Surau Kampung Sungai Pusu is a musolla located at Kampung Sungai Pusu, Gombak. It was built around the 1980s and was renovated until how it is now today. It is a surau under the qariah of Masjid Zakaria. Although it is small, it still keeps the community in Kampung Sungai Pusu strong and united. This website serves to ease the work of the surau management community and also the muslim community that uses the surau. Through this website, the management can update upcoming events that are going to be held in the surau. The public community could also contact or check the contact details of the surau through this website. This website can also gives the facilities that the surau has.
